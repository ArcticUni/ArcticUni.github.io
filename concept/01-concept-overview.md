# ArcticUNI V2 — Concept Overview

## What we're building

A new ArcticUNI website built with **Astro + Tailwind CSS**, using the same technical
scaffolding as marinesparkx.github.io. The goal is a premium, Nordic editorial feel
that matches the quality of the product itself.

## Why rebuild instead of patch the current site?

The current `ArcticUNI.github.io` site is plain HTML — one large file, no image
optimization, styling copy-pasted by hand. It works, but:

- Images are uncompressed (slow on mobile)
- Hard to maintain as content grows
- Visual identity is generic (Inter font, mixed light/dark sections)
- Doesn't share the Marine Spark X design language

The Astro rebuild gives us image compression for free, reusable components, and a
visual identity that clearly sits inside the Marine Spark X family.

## Target audience (brainstorm — needs your input)

Based on the South Korea onepager, ArcticUNI is aimed at:
- B2B: Korean supplement importers / distributors / retailers
- B2C: Premium health supplement consumers who care about origin and traceability

**Question for you:** Is this site primarily for B2B partners or end consumers?
That changes a lot — B2B means focus on certifications, MOQ, compliance.
B2C means focus on storytelling, lifestyle imagery, why-it-works.

## Core message (brainstorm)

Current tagline: "From the Arctic Ocean to You"

Possible alternatives:
- "The Purest Calcium on Earth" (product-led)
- "69°N. Traceable. Pure." (origin + credential-led)
- "Where Arctic Waters Meet Precision Science" (premium science angle)
- Keep "From the Arctic Ocean to You" — it's clear and warm

**Question for you:** What feeling should someone have 5 seconds after landing on the page?

## Relationship to marinesparkx.github.io

ArcticUNI is a Marine Spark X product. The V2 site should:
- Feel like a sibling brand (same typographic family, similar color palette)
- But have its own identity — ArcticUNI is the *consumer/partner-facing* product,
  Marine Spark X is the company behind it
- Cross-link to marinesparkx.github.io (e.g. footer: "A Marine Spark X Company")
